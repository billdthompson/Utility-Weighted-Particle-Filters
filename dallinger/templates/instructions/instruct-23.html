Instructions 1/3

You work for a company cleaning a chemical recently spilled in a protected ocean zone. You will observe areas briefly and decide if the areas contain more chemical or more water. An areas has more chemical if there are more yellow dots than blue, and more water if there are more blue dots than yellow. Note that the size of the dots does not matter, only the number.
