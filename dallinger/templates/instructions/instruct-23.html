Instructions

Your job at the company is to extract oil deposits in a desert area. You have already been payed for your work. You will observe areas only briefly and decide if the area is good or bad. Each area has black circles of oil and yellow circles of sand. An area is good for you if there is more sand than oil, and bad for if there is more oil than sand. For each area you get a $0.25 bonus if you are correct. Areas with more oil are bad because they are expensive to extract -- you must spend $0.25 of the money you've been to payed to extract the oil in every bad area you find.
