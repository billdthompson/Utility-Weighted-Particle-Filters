Instructions

Your job at the company is to explore oil deposits in a desert area. You will observe areas only briefly and decide if the area is good or bad. Each area has black circles of oil and yellow circles of sand. An area is good if there is more oil than sand, and bad if there is more sand than oil. For each area you get a $0.25 bonus if you are correct. Also, if the area has more oil than sand, you and all employees get an additional $0.25 bonus from the company's profits.
